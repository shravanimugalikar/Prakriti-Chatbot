What is Ayurveda?

Ayurveda is an ancient Indian health system focused on balancing your body and mind. It teaches that everything, including us, is made of five elements: Earth, Water, Fire, Air, and Ether.

These elements combine to form three unique energies in your body called Doshas: Vata, Pitta, and Kapha. Your unique mix of these Doshas is called your Prakriti, defining your natural constitution.

The Three Doshas

Vata

Elements: Air & Ether

Governs: Movement, breathing, circulation.
Traits: Quick, creative, energetic. Can be anxious or restless when out of balance.

Pitta

Elements: Fire & Water

Governs: Digestion, metabolism, intelligence.
Traits: Focused, intelligent, intense. Can be irritable or prone to anger when out of balance.

Kapha

Elements: Earth & Water

Governs: Structure, lubrication, stability.
Traits: Calm, stable, loving. Can be lethargic or resistant to change when out of balance.

Balancing Your Doshas

Ayurveda suggests adjusting your lifestyle, diet, and habits to bring your Doshas into harmony.

  • Vata: Focus on warmth, routine, and moist, grounding foods.
  • Pitta: Emphasize cooling foods, moderation, and relaxation.
  • Kapha: Prioritize stimulating activities, light foods, and regular exercise.
